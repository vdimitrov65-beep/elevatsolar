03 · BESS tracker
Storage moving from pipeline to operation in EU markets
Bulgaria · Commissioned
ContourGlobal 202 MW / 500 MWh - Stara Zagora
Online since January 2026. Standalone BESS, one of the largest in continental Europe. Participating in day-ahead and intraday markets.
Greece · Regulatory shift
Hellenic Energy Exchange admits BESS to spot markets
2 April 2026. First battery participation in Greek spot markets. Priority Regime fast-tracks grid connection for up to 4.7 GW of standalone storage, no operational subsidies.
Romania · In development
1.2+ GW standalone BESS pipeline
Enery: 1+ GWh BESS co-located with 761 MWp solar. Nuvve + Omnia Global: 60 MW / 120 MWh in Brasov (COD Q4 2026). Renalfa Power Clusters acquired two additional projects.
Serbia · Pipeline
Fortis Energy: 270 MWp + 72 MWh BESS, Sremska Mitrovica
Construction permit in hand. Start Q2 2026. Will be among the largest solar plus storage projects in SEE.
Price signal: Bulgaria IBEX day-ahead reached 136.6 EUR/MWh on 2 April, up from 67.5 EUR/MWh on 27 February. Linked to the Middle East conflict and gas price shock. A direct argument for BESS arbitrage in the Bulgarian market.

05 · Policy watch
Grid reform in Romania, BESS framework in Greece, new energy law in N. Macedonia
Romania
Transelectrica's new grid allocation rules effective 2026: annual competitive auctions for connection capacity at projects above 5 MW. Auction revenues fund grid reinforcement. Romania moves to an EU-standard grid access model.
Greece
Priority Regime for BESS allocates 4,700 MW of grid connection for standalone storage with no operational subsidies. Revenue is market-based. The industry puts the number needed to address curtailment at 8 GW.
N. Macedonia
New Energy Law (May 2025) now in effect: annual energy infrastructure plan, storage as a separate regulated activity, household PV limit raised from 6 kW to 10 kW. Ministry approved 59 PV projects (3+ GW) and 96 storage facilities (2.2 GW / 4.9 GWh) for 2026.
BiH / Geopolitics
Republika Srpska offering BAM 400M in guarantees for coal capacity - a clear counter-signal to the energy transition. Greece's energy minister stated Serbia cannot be excluded from the Vertical Gas Corridor for US LNG.
06 · Elevat angle
BESS bankability: the dispatch gap is still open
Greece opened its BESS market on 2 April. That is good news. The problem is that most battery offers we see across SEE still do not include a dispatch strategy in the term sheet. A 200 MW battery without a revenue model is expensive infrastructure, not a bankable asset. The PPC-Metlen JV and Romania's 40 EUR/MWh CfD pricing are forcing developers to sharpen their numbers. Projects with a clear dispatch logic - arbitrage, FCR, PPA firming - will get financed. The rest will wait.
